Easy Baked Chicken Thighs

Juicy Baked Chicken Thighs: A Flavorful Classic

Indulge in our Juicy Baked Chicken Thighs, a recipe that's both simple and delicious. These chicken thighs are seasoned to perfection and baked until they're tender and juicy, with a crispy skin that's full of flavor. The blend of herbs and spices adds a savory touch, making each bite a mouthwatering experience. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal, this dish pairs beautifully with your favorite sides and is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons onion flakes

How To Make It

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
